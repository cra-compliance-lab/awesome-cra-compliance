## What Is The CRA?

The EU Cyber Resilience Act (CRA) is an EU regulation introducing cybersecurity requirements for products with digital elements across their lifecycle. It covers areas such as secure-by-design expectations, vulnerability handling, security updates, technical documentation, conformity assessment, and market surveillance.

This repository is organized to make CRA compliance research easier, especially for people searching for:

- CRA Official Journal text
- CRA standards and harmonised standards
- CRA vulnerability handling and reporting
- CRA CE marking and conformity assessment
- CRA open source obligations
- CRA manufacturer, importer, and distributor obligations

- `OJ` - Official Journal of the European Union.
